Shipping Policy


At our plant selling website, we deliver only within India. We use trusted courier services and Indian Post to send your courier to ensure the safe and timely delivery of your product. You can expect to receive your product within 5 to 7 days of placing your order.

Please note that we do not dispatch items on national holidays and weekends (Saturday and Sunday). This means that the delivery time may be extended if your order is placed on a holiday or weekend. However, we always aim to get your product to you as soon as possible, and we will provide you with a tracking number so that you can keep an eye on your delivery.
